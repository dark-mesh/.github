# DarkMesh 🕸️

We are **DarkMesh**.

You found us because you know — and reject — the lack of clarity and trust elsewhere.

DarkMesh is a small collective building software **for everyone, and among everyone**.
It is not a company.
It is a mesh: distributed, open, and shared.

Build with us.
Leave a legacy for the community.
Once written, always remembered.

## Structure

DarkMesh is organized as a mesh with **8 seats**.

Seats are not status.
They are responsibility.

Each seat may form teams and projects independently, while remaining connected through shared values and communication.

All seats are currently empty.
