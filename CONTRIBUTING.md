# Contributing to DarkMesh

DarkMesh is open by design and selective by intent.

You do not need permission to build.
You do need responsibility to stay.

---

## Ways to Contribute

You can contribute by:
- building software
- maintaining existing projects
- reviewing code
- improving documentation
- participating in discussions

Contribution is measured by output and clarity, not activity.

---

## Expectations

All contributors are expected to:

- Communicate
  - Share intent, direction, and progress
  - Notify others when making breaking or risky changes

- Build for the public
  - Prioritize openness and accessibility
  - Avoid extractive or closed practices

- Respect autonomy
  - Teams own their work
  - Disagreements are resolved through discussion, not authority

- Act responsibly
  - Consider security, privacy, and safety
  - Escalate concerns early

---

## Joining a Team

Teams form organically under seats.

To join a team:
- engage with the work
- communicate consistently
- demonstrate alignment with the DarkMesh spirit

There are no formal onboarding steps.
Trust is earned through action.

---

## Becoming a Seat Holder

Seat holders are invited, not elected.

If you believe you are aligned:
- contribute consistently
- communicate openly
- demonstrate long-term intent

Invitations are extended when alignment is clear.

---

## What Is Not Accepted

- silence without notice
- misrepresentation of DarkMesh
- illegal activity
- actions that harm the public or the mesh

Freedom ends where harm begins.

---

## Leaving

You may leave at any time.

Your contributions remain:
- open
- forkable
- part of the DarkMesh history

Once written, always remembered.
