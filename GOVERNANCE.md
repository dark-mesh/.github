# DarkMesh Governance

DarkMesh is a mesh, not a hierarchy.

Governance exists to preserve the spirit, continuity, and clarity of the mesh — not to control its participants.

---

## Founder & Stewardship

DarkMesh was founded with a clear vision and is stewarded to preserve that vision.

The founder acts as **Steward**, responsible for:
- protecting the DarkMesh spirit
- resolving conflicts that threaten the mesh
- intervening only when necessary

Stewardship is not micromanagement.
Autonomy is the default.

---

## The 8 Seats and Teams

DarkMesh consists of **8 seats**, represented by the mesh 🕸️.

Each seat corresponds to **exactly one team**.
The number of seats and their names are fixed.

The eight teams are:

- **ProtocolZero**
- **PeerLab**
- **Entropy**
- **Shadowbit**
- **Coldstone**
- **Deephash**
- **Numbrella**
- **CalmRoute**

Seats are not status.
Seats are responsibility.

Each seat:
- owns one team
- may grow that team to any size
- may internally organize into sub-teams
- is fully accountable for the actions and outcomes of that team

Seats are peers.
No seat governs another.

---

## Claiming a Seat

Seats are claimed through **invitation and mutual agreement**.

The process:
1. The Steward or an existing seat holder extends an invitation.
2. A conversation takes place (text or audio) with the Steward.
   - This is not a company interview.
   - It exists to understand intent, values, and expectations.
3. If both sides agree, the seat — and its corresponding team — is assigned.

There are no applications.
There is no competition.
Trust is built through clarity.

---

## Meetings & Communication

DarkMesh values regular, lightweight coordination.

### Seat Meetings
- Monthly
- Participants: the 8 seat holders
- Format: text or audio
- Purpose: alignment, visibility, early conflict detection

### Mesh Meetings
- Every 3 months
- Participants: seats and their teams
- Purpose: sharing progress, direction, and lessons

### Public Meetings
- Every 6 months
- Open to the public
- Purpose: transparency, accountability, and knowledge sharing

Attendance is expected.
Silence is not acceptable.

---

## Responsibility & Liability

Each team and seat is fully responsible for its actions.

DarkMesh:
- does not shield illegal activity
- does not absorb legal responsibility for teams
- does not provide anonymity for harm

All work must comply with applicable laws.
Violation may result in removal from the mesh.

---

## Intervention

Intervention occurs only when:
- communication stops
- the DarkMesh spirit is violated
- harm to the community or public trust is caused

The goal of intervention is stability, not punishment.

---

## Steward Succession

The Steward role is singular and non-executive.

If the current Steward exits:
- a new Steward is selected from among existing seat holders
- the outgoing Steward selects the successor by default
- if the outgoing Steward cannot or chooses not to decide, the seats may select a Steward by simple majority

Upon becoming Steward:
- the individual relinquishes their seat
- their former seat becomes vacant
- the Steward holds no seats

The Steward does not own projects or teams.
The role exists solely to preserve the DarkMesh spirit and continuity.

---

DarkMesh is designed to outlive any individual.
